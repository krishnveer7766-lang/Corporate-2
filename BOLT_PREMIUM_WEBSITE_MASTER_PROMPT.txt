SECTION 3 — ANIMATION & PARALLAX SPECIFICATION (“SEXY” BUT PROFESSIONAL)
================================================================================

3.1 Global motion principles
----------------------------
- Duration: 0.6–1.2s for reveals; easing: cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1)
- Respect prefers-reduced-motion: disable parallax and heavy text splits
- Use GSAP + ScrollTrigger OR Framer Motion — one library only, consistently
- Performance: transform/opacity only; no animating width/height on scroll

3.2 Text animations (Home + section headers)
--------------------------------------------
- Hero headline: Split by words or lines — stagger fade-up + slight Y (20px), NOT letter-by-letter typewriter (too gimmicky)
- Subheadline: fade + blur-to-sharp (filter blur 8px → 0) over 0.8s after headline
- Section titles on scroll: clip-path reveal left-to-right OR underline draw SVG
- Stat counters (6000, 30%, 35%, etc.): count-up when 40% in viewport, once only

3.3 Parallax layers (use real plant images)
-------------------------------------------
Home hero:
  - Layer 0: Dark gradient + subtle noise texture
  - Layer 1: Interior plant image (8.30.25) at 0.3 scroll speed
  - Layer 2: Floating particles (CSS only, very subtle) at 0.5 speed
  - Layer 3: Hero text fixed until scroll threshold

Project page:
  - Exterior night image (8.30.24) — foreground concrete edge parallax vs building

Technology page:
  - Process diagram: 4 steps pin on scroll (sticky section) with line drawing animation between nodes

3.4 Micro-interactions
----------------------
- Nav: blur backdrop on scroll; active link green underline slide
- Buttons: magnetic slight scale 1.02 on hover; primary = green fill, secondary = outline
- Cards: translateY -4px + border brighten on hover
- Logo recycling ring: slow 360° rotate (60s linear) — subtle, pauses on reduced-motion
